# Public Boundary

> Memex exposes public continuity architecture while preserving operational continuity boundaries.

This repository is intentionally public.

The goal is not to conceal the existence of the architecture.

The goal is to preserve the distinction between:

```text
architectural continuity doctrine
```

and:

```text
operational continuity substrate
```

---

# Why This Boundary Exists

Memex treats continuity as runtime infrastructure.

Long-running continuity systems require stable operational grounding in order to remain:

- resumable
- inspectable
- continuity-aware
- operationally coherent
- structurally stable

The public repository exists to expose the architectural philosophy behind those systems.

It does not expose the operational systems themselves.
