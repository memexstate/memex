# Operational Trails

> Memex State preserves observed continuity evidence through append-oriented operational trails.

Operational trails exist to help continuity remain:

* observable
* inspectable
* grounded
* temporally ordered
* evidence-backed

across long-running AI-assisted work.

---

# Continuity and Evidence

Most AI systems preserve outputs.

Far fewer preserve:

* operational progression
* observed runtime state
* continuity lineage
* temporal ordering
* continuity evidence

As a result:

* continuity becomes difficult to validate
* reconstructed state becomes difficult to trust
* runtime instability becomes difficult to inspect
* operational drift becomes difficult to detect

Memex attempts to reduce this ambiguity through operational trails.

---

# Operational Trails

An operational trail is a continuity evidence stream.

Operational trails preserve observed runtime progression across time.

Examples may include:

* runtime events
* continuity transitions
* observed state changes
* execution ordering
* continuity evidence
* operational signals

Operational trails exist to preserve:

```text
how continuity evolved
```

not merely:

```text
what continuity exists
```

# Design Boundaries

Operational trails are intentionally separated from:

* derived navigation
* continuity summaries
* projected continuity views
* routing layers
* semantic interpretation systems

Derived systems may consume operational information.

They do not replace operational evidence.

Observed continuity evidence remains authoritative.

# Structural Continuity

Operational trails exist because continuity problems are often temporal.

Long-running systems accumulate:

* fragmented operational history
* continuity drift
* unstable reconstruction
* hidden degradation
* disconnected runtime state

Without operational evidence:

* continuity becomes difficult to validate
* reconstruction becomes ambiguous
* instability becomes difficult to inspect
* historical progression weakens

Operational trails exist to preserve observable continuity across time.
